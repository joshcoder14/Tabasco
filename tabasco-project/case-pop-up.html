Wox

#identity

#illustration

Wox is a moving box manufacturing and rental company. One of the main advantages is that the company cares about the environment by offering to rent plastic boxes for rent instead of using disposable cardboard boxes that will later be thrown away or burnt.

The idea of the logo is simple, it is a literal representation of the box, and the cyclical animation of the logo reflects the idea of recycling. A communication style and company website were also developed. The main idea of which is that the media of communication or the site themselves are a box and we show everything that can be put inside. Using different elements thereby we address different groups of customers.
